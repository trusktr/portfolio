-- http://trusktr.io ↴

Here you can see my random password generator as well as information about me. The password generator takes the user's random mouse movement to enhance seeding during random number generation. The generator also makes use of discrete Blum Blum Shub sequences. A Blum Blum Shub sequence is a sequence of numbers that have no apparent pattern. The formula is

X[n] = X[n-1]^2 % M

where M is a product of two arbitrary primes (the bigger the better) and the first number in the sequence, X[0], is arbitrarily chosen.
